Timeline

Multi-Track Personal Productivity Application

In Development - Architecture Phase

Project Overview

Timeline reimagines personal productivity through a revolutionary "video editor" style multi-track interface. Unlike traditional single-track calendars, it allows users to visualize and manage parallel activities across multiple tracks simultaneously, providing a more realistic representation of modern multi-threaded workflows.

Target User

"User Zero" - The Unserved Achiever: Highly motivated individuals who have abandoned traditional productivity tools because they fail to support multi-threaded workflows.
