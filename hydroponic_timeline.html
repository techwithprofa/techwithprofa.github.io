Project Overview

This project demonstrates a hydroponic system for Nai Miris (Sri Lankan scotch bonnet), a high-yield chili. The journey is structured into two phases: a successful running prototype and an upcoming IoT-powered commercial expansion.

  • Expand to 50 plants for commercial scale
