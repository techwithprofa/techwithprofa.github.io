Hybrid Bicycle BMS

Smart Battery Management with Energy Recovery & Home Assistant Integration

Project Completed

Project Overview

This project involved leading the software and electrical engineering efforts to develop a smart battery management system (BMS) and sensor network for a hybrid foot bicycle. We integrated ESP32 microcontrollers with Home Assistant for real-time control and automation. The key challenge was controlling the battery pack at the single-cell level to optimize energy recovery and overall system performance, a goal achieved within a 4-week timeframe.
